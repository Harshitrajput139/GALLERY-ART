Krishna Lifting up Mount Govardhan

Artist: Shahadin
Medium: Traditional Painting
Theme: Devotion and Divine Protection

KNOW MORE
This artwork shows Lord Krishna, as a young boy, lifting Mount Govardhan to shelter the villagers and animals of Vrindavan from torrential rains. It symbolizes faith, courage, and divine love, reminding us how one selfless act can protect an entire community.

Rama Bestows His Possessions on the Brahmins, His Friends, and Servants, Folio from the "Shangri"

Origin: Folio from the "Shangri" manuscript

Medium: Ink, Color, and Gold on Paper
Theme: Generosity, Duty, and Dharma

KNOW MORE
This folio illustrates the moment when Lord Rama, before beginning his exile, gives away his wealth and possessions to the Brahmins, his loyal friends, and faithful servants. It reflects the ideals of dharma (righteousness), charity, and selfless sacrifice — the values that made Rama a beloved king and a guiding light for humanity

Ravana, Overwhelmed by Grief on Hearing of His Son's Death


Theme: War, Loss, and Vengeance
Medium: Traditional Epic Painting
Source: Ramayana Illustration

KNOW MORE

This painting captures the moment when Ravana, the mighty king of Lanka, is devastated by the news of his son’s death in battle. Overcome with sorrow, yet burning with rage, he sends out more of his forces to fight against Lord Rama. a powerful reminder of how grief and anger can shape destiny — a turning point in the epic war of the Ramayana.

Gallery Guidelines: How to Truly Experience Art

  1. Pause and Breathe: Take a moment before each artwork to let your mind settle.
  2. Observe the Details: Notice colors, textures, and expressions you might otherwise miss.
  3. Feel the Story: Imagine the story or emotion the artist wants to convey.
  4. Ask Questions: Wonder why the artist chose this scene, pose, or palette.
  5. Share Your Thoughts: Discuss with friends or jot down your reflections.
  6. Respect the Art: Never touch the artworks and maintain a quiet, attentive presence.
  7. Leave Inspired: Let each piece spark a new idea or feeling inside you.
